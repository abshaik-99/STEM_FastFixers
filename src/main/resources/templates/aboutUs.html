About STEM FastFixers: Your Home Service Ally

Let us be your first phone call…

At STEM FastFixers, we've got a unique and budget-friendly way to make life easier for busy adults and seniors. We're here to provide premium, trustworthy handyman services, take care of your home inside and out, and offer specialized senior modifications to keep your home safe.

Our goal? To help you keep your home in tip-top shape. From fixing odds and ends to keeping your place well-maintained year-round, and even making those senior-friendly changes, we've got you covered. And the best part? You can trust us to feel safe. Hey Seniors!

We are certified Senior Safety Specialists helping you stay in your home longer with comfort, safety and dignity.
